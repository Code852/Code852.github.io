Imagine coming home to unwind on your private balcony and enjoy breathtaking 180-degree ocean views extending to Catalina Island! Ideally set on an elevated lot near popular destinations, this stunning Laguna Beach gem treats you to a dream coastal lifestyle. Across the sunlit interior, discover a beautiful combination of crisp white tones, travertine tile flooring, and expansive glass sliders that frame inspiring panoramas. Allowing a seamless flow, the open-concept layout has a generously sized living room where you can gather with guests around the tile-surround fireplace. Steps away from the dining area, the kitchen equips the home chef with stainless steel appliances, handsome wood cabinetry, plus matching granite countertops and backsplash. Versatile, well-sized bedrooms await downstairs, including the primary suite adorned by a fireplace and large doors that whisk you to a tropical deck with a soothing hot tub. A tastefully tiled ensuite immerses you in spa-like relaxation with a double vanity and an enclosed walk-in shower. One of the best spots to relish sensational sunsets is on the oversized balcony as you sip a refreshing nightcap around the fire pit. Completing this remarkable haven’s appeal is an attached 2-car garage, a lushly landscaped backyard, plus close proximity to Moulton Meadows Park, Victoria Beach, local shops, and restaurants. Why just read about this must-see residence when you can experience it yourself? Come for a tour while you still can!
